Call us to confirm current pricing or ask about daily specials. GOOD TO KNOW Frequently Asked Questions Do you offer delivery to my area? We deliver throughout Boothwyn and the surrounding Delco area. Give us a call at (610) 497-1230 and we can confirm whether your address is within range. Is there a delivery minimum? Yes, most delivery orders have a minimum order amount. Call ahead and we'll let you know the current minimum for your address. Do you take credit or debit cards? Yes, we accept all major credit and debit cards in addition to cash, both for pickup and delivery orders. How long does a typical order take? Most takeout orders are ready in 15–25 minutes depending on how busy we are. Delivery times vary based on distance and order volume — we're happy to give you an estimate when you call. Can I order online instead of calling? We're best reached by phone for the fastest service, but we're also available through major delivery apps — see the options below. Do you have indoor seating? Yes, we have limited indoor seating for guests who'd like to dine in. Since space is limited, most guests choose takeout or delivery — but you're always welcome to stop in.
