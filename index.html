Our Story

Casual, Honest, Homemade

Since 2014, China Town has proudly served the Boothwyn community with fresh Chinese favorites, generous portions, and friendly service. We're a neighborhood restaurant where customers know they can count on a good meal at a fair price.

Our pork fried rice has become a customer favorite, especially when paired with one of our hand-rolled egg rolls or a hot bowl of our homemade hot and sour soup. We keep things simple: traditional recipes, freshly prepared, and served with care.

Whether you're picking up dinner on the way home or stopping in for a quick bite, we're here to make every visit easy and enjoyable. Most of our business is takeout, but we also have limited seating if you'd like to dine with us.

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery to my area?

We deliver throughout Boothwyn and the surrounding Delco area. Give us a call at (610) 497-1230 and we can confirm whether your address is within range.

Is there a delivery minimum?

Yes, most delivery orders have a minimum order amount. Call ahead and we'll let you know the current minimum for your address.

Do you take credit or debit cards?

Yes, we accept all major credit and debit cards in addition to cash, both for pickup and delivery orders.

How long does a typical order take?

Most takeout orders are ready in 15–25 minutes depending on how busy we are. Delivery times vary based on distance and order volume — we're happy to give you an estimate when you call.

Can I order online instead of calling?

We're best reached by phone for the fastest service, but we're also available through major delivery apps — see the options below.

Do you have indoor seating?

Yes, we have limited indoor seating for guests who'd like to dine in. Since space is limited, most guests choose takeout or delivery — but you're always welcome to stop in.
